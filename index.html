The Sobiverse

Enter the Sobiverse if you dare...


I've always been an avid writer but I've never created a blog before so I decided to do so.

I think writing is more powerful than the Hulk and Thor's Mjölnir hammer combined. I think everyone's thoughts are cool and significant, someone could one day be the inventor of a time machine or figure out a way to record and play back dreams or discover how to eat a bowl of tomato soup using telekinesis. Writing and reading give you the ability to take your mind somewhere impossible. I hope you enjoy this but if it's not your cup of chai, you're welcome to delve into your own fictional universe. I'm sure it'll be more thrilling than mine anyway.


You don't need to dilute your imagination like it's Robinson's, orange-flavoured-single-strength-diluting juice. Drink it with no water to activate your creativity.
